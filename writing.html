I am passionate about writing. In my free time, I write both fiction and nonfiction essays. Links to my work are embedded in the titles:

Fiction: The Arrow

"The Arrow," is fictional piece is centered on a boy who goes hunting alone for the first time.

Fiction: Smile

"Smile," is another fictional piece that explores the lingering effects of an old relationship.

Fiction: Creak

In "Creak," the acts of intrusion and numbing unfold in a mini-drama between an intruder and oblivious girl.

Fiction: I Have a Bomb in My Purse/Pills

These are two fiction stories I wrote for my introductory creative writing class. The first one, "I Have a Bomb in My Purse", follows a disgruntled woman into a bridal shop. The second piece, "Pills", is a portrayal of a Munchousen by Proxy relationship.

Longform Creative Nonfiction: My Body for Your Consumption
